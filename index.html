What is Mi?

Mi is your electronic pet and is also yourself! It will grow up as you exercise more, and become weak if you stop exercising. It can interact with you in your daily life as a real pet. The more you walk, the stronger your Mi will be. Exercise to be fit, grow up your Mi, and combat with others!
